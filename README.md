# ApplyWave

**ApplyWave** is an AI-powered admissions friction detector designed to help students understand where they are getting stuck in the college application process and what they should do next.

Most admissions platforms only show missing items or application status. ApplyWave goes further by identifying applicant blockers, surfacing incomplete steps, and turning confusion into a practical action plan.

It is built for two main users:

- **Applicants**, who want to know what they have finished, what is missing, and what to prioritize next
- **Institutions**, which want anonymized insights into where applicants commonly struggle across the admissions funnel

---

## Problem

Many students do not stop applying because they are unqualified.  
They stop because they feel overwhelmed, confused, delayed, or unsure what matters most.

Common friction points include:

- not knowing how to start essays
- delaying recommendation requests
- confusion around testing or documents
- uncertainty about financial aid steps
- extra complexity for international applicants

Admissions teams also face a related problem: they often cannot clearly see where students are getting stuck before those students drop off.

ApplyWave was created to make that friction visible.

---

## Solution

ApplyWave helps students answer four simple questions:

- What have I already completed?
- What is still missing?
- What is actually blocking me?
- What should I do next?

The product guides students through a structured application check-in, identifies their main blocker, and returns a personalized checklist and next-step plan.

On the institution side, ApplyWave can also surface anonymized patterns such as:

- common blocker categories
- high-friction stages in the application journey
- risk distribution across applicants
- differences in challenges for domestic and international applicants

---

## Core Features

### Applicant Flow

Applicants can:

- choose whether they are applying to **Minerva** or **other colleges**
- enter the colleges they are applying to
- mark which parts of the application they have already completed
- explain what they are stuck on
- get a personalized result based on their current application state

### Smart Friction Detection

ApplyWave identifies the student’s likely blocker, such as:

- essay uncertainty
- recommendation delay
- document confusion
- deadline management
- testing gap
- financial aid uncertainty
- international requirements confusion
- general overwhelm

### Results Page

The results page gives students:

- a summary of what they have already accomplished
- a list of what is still incomplete
- their current main blocker
- a practical checklist
- one best next action
- a short priority plan

### Institution Dashboard

The institution side is separated from the student experience and focuses on anonymized aggregate insights, such as:

- top blocker categories
- stage-by-stage friction
- distribution of applicant risk
- trends across applicant groups

## Why ApplyWave Matters

ApplyWave is not just a checklist tool and not just a chatbot.

Its main value is this:

**it detects friction before it becomes applicant drop-off.**

That makes it useful not only for students, but also for admissions teams trying to improve applicant experience and completion rates.

This matters especially for:

- first-generation applicants
- international applicants
- students applying to multiple schools
- students who feel unsure how to prioritize tasks

---

## Product Vision

The long-term vision for ApplyWave is to become a guidance layer for admissions.

Instead of showing students a static portal, ApplyWave creates a more responsive experience that helps them move through the application process with clarity.

For institutions, the vision is to provide a better understanding of where process design, communication, or requirements may be creating unnecessary friction.

## Example User Journey

1. A student opens ApplyWave  
2. They choose Minerva or other colleges  
3. They enter what they have already completed  
4. They explain what feels difficult  
5. ApplyWave diagnoses the main blocker  
6. The student receives a checklist, priorities, and next step guidance

This makes the application process feel more manageable and less overwhelming.

---

## Privacy Note

ApplyWave is designed with applicant trust in mind.

Any institution-facing analytics are intended to use **anonymous, aggregate data only** to help improve applicant experience and understand common friction points.

Student-facing experiences should clearly support the applicant first.
